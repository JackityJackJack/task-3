Metaverse

What if the internet could be translated into a virtual reality context? Concepts and ideas around the Metaverse explore this. Virtual reality worlds already exist but current hopes are to combine them together to essentially make a network worlds, essentially an interation of the internet where we will become even more connected through more naturally feeling interactions facilitated by digital avatars. Technology to interact in this new internet will link more with our physiology, such as todays VR headsets in which you physically move your head to explore.

Alcor & Cryonics

Forever have we pushed closer to the boundaries of our physical bodies lifespan. I argue the idea of immortality or an extended life has always exsisted in human culture proven in dispositions such as thanataphobi, the fear of death. A more physical and closely related example is cryonics where the dying process is slowed by ultra-low temperatures. Could digitalisation be our ultimate escape from death?

Internet of Things

Classifys the transformation of previously non-web related items into network components. The modern household serves as a the qunitessential microcosm for the phenomena in which ammenties such as your fridge is linked to your network. This allows multiple ammenties to be accessed, operated and managed through one's device giving home owners the ease that comes with the connectedness of the Internet. Iot along with the metaverse further blurs the borders between the physical and digital world as they become ever more dependent on each other and ever more intergrated.

Bionics, Elon Musk & Neuralink

Elon Musks' company Nueralink develops neuroelectricphysiological recording devices that can listen to neurons on 1024 channels. In a recent 2021 'Monkey MindPong' it is demonstrated the nueral link can be paired to an iphone, 'our goal is for a person with paralysis to use a computer or phone with their brain activity alone'. This is one of the most literal contemporary examples of our brains intergration with the web as signals from the neuralink are relayed wirelessly to control other devices as in the demo.
